Special Notice

To all dear customers,

According to the updated instructions of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region Government on the prevention and control of the "COVID-19", all branches of ABC Beauty Salon will be temporarily closed from January 7, 2022 (Friday) until further notice from the government.

The customer service hotline will maintains normal services.

For affected guests during this period, please call 2903 2841 to change the appointment.

If there is any further news or updated information, it will be announced on the official website of ABC Beauty Salon.

Sorry for any inconveniences caused.

ABC Beauty Salon fights the virus together with everyone and wishes everyone good health!

Staff protections

During the epidemic, to protect the safety of customers and employees, customers who come to the store to enjoy beauty services need to wear masks at all times and receive at least one dose of the new crown vaccine.

Customers must use "LeaveHomeSafe", and staff and customers must wear masks throughout the process.

Our employees are regularly tested for COVID-19 to ensure a safe and hygienic work environment.
